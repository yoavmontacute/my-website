I am a postdoctoral researcher at the NII (国立情報学研究所).

Research focus: logic and foundations of mathematics, theory of computation, algorithms.

Academic interests: mathematical logic, category theory, dynamical systems, topology, algebraic topology, finite model theory, graph theory.

論理学・数学基礎論・計算理論の研究。
